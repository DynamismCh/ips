ABOUT US

CW College Pty (Ltd) was founded by Machuene M Ranoto. A truck driving veteran, in June 2017 after 29 years on the road. I wanted to help people understand the trucking industry and everything that comes with the career and lifestyle of an over the road driver. I want to help you make the right choices and prepare for a great start to your trucking career.

MISSION STATEMENT

Produce well-trained entry-level truck drivers who can transition into gainful employment in their respective fields upon completion of the vocational training objectives.

VISION

  • CW College aspires to be a vocational institution which serves at a level of national quality by maintaining our long-term trend-setting role within the trucking industries.
  • CW College strives to excel and exceed the industry standard for vocational training which will allow our graduates to achieve gainful employment.
  • CW College will continue to provide distinctive training programs for truck drivers.
